FromSoftware Tribute Page

The studio who brought gaming back

The Development team of FromSoftware at work on Dark Souls III

Here is a timeline of FromSoftware, Inc.

  • 1986 - FromSoftware, Inc. is established in Sasazuka, Shibuya-ku, Tokyo, for the development of business application software.
  • 1994 - First venture into the game software development market in response to SONY´s launch of its game console, PlayStation. 3D real-time RPG "KING'S FIELD (PS)" is released.
  • 1997 - 3D mech battle simulation game "ARMORED CORE (PS)" is released. Solidifies their foundation for video game development through their 3D virtual world creation skills.
  • 1999 - July: Enters the Sega Dreamcast software market with the release of multiplayer mech action game "FRAME GRIDE (DC)."
  • 2000 - Joins the SONY PlayStation 2 software market. March: 3D real-time RPG "ETERNAL RING (PS2)" is released. August: 3D mech action game "ARMORED CORE 2 (PS2)" is released.
  • 2002 -Begins developing and releasing titles for the Nintendo GameCube and the Microsoft Xbox. First game development for multiple platforms. April: Card-based action RPG "RUNE (GC)" is released. July: High-speed 3D mech action game "MURAKUMO (Xbox)" is released.
  • 2003 - April: 3D ninja action game "TENCHU 3 (PS2)" is released. This is the first time that FromSoftware publishes software developed by another studio.
  • 2004 - Enters the mobile gaming market. January: FromSoftware's first mobile phone game, "KING'S FIELD" is released. July: Acquires the copyright of the Tenchu series from Activision Publishing, Inc. December: Enters the SONY PlayStation Portable software market with the release of the mech simulator "ARMORED CORE FORMULA FRONT (PSP)." December: Marks the 10th anniversary of the entry into the game software market.
  • 2005 - January: The first game developed jointly with Banpresto Co., Ltd., "Another Century’s Episode (PS2)" is released. August: The 10th installment in the series, "ARMORED CORE LAST RAVEN (PS2)" is released. September: Hosts the industry's first internship that lets students experience game development, using our own PSP adventure game creation kit "ADVENTURE PLAYER (PSP)."
  • 2006 - Enters the Microsoft Xbox 360 software market. January: Role-playing game "[eM]-eNCHANT arM- (Xbox 360)" is released. March: The second game developed jointly with Banpresto Co., Ltd., "Another Century’s Episode 2 (PS2)" is released. April: Enters the Nintendo NINTENDO DS software market with the release of "TENCHU DARK SHADOW (NDS)." June: Develops a game jointly with Sega Corporation and releases the team-based online action game "CHROMEHOUNDS (Xbox 360)." July and September: Increases capital through allocation of new shares to a third party. November: Marks the 20th anniversary of the establishment of the company. "ARMORED CORE -MACHINE SIDE BOX- 1997-2006" and "KING'S FIELD DARK SIDE BOX" are released on the 20th anniversary. December: Enters the SONY PlayStation 3 software market with the release of the high-speed mech action game, "ARMORED CORE 4 (PS3)."
  • 2007 - Jointly establishes Co-Core Inc. with transcosmos inc. and SANKEI SHIMBUN CO., LTD. Announces the development of the 3D virtual community project, "meet-me." July: "Hello Kitty NO OSHARE PARTY SANRIO CHARACTER ZUKAN DS (NDS)" is released in collaboration with Sanrio Co., Ltd. August: "BIKKURIMAN DAIJITEN (NDS)" is released in collaboration with LOTTE Co., Ltd. September: Jointly develops the third game with Banpresto Co., Ltd. and releases "Another Century’s Episode 3 THE FINAL (PS2)".
  • 2008 - May: Undergoes a stock split. October: Enters the Nintendo Wii software market with the release of the ninja action game "TENCHU 4".
  • 2009 - February: FromSoftware works with Sony for the first time and develops the action RPG "Demon's Souls (PS3)" which is released by Sony Interactive Entertainment. June (to March 2010): 6 PSP remakes are released as the Portable series. September: "Demon's Souls" is selected as one of the Award for Excellence winners for Japan Game Award 2009.
  • 2010 - August: "Another Century's Episode: R (PS3)" is released as the fourth game developed jointly with Banpresto Co., Ltd. August: FromSoftware works with Capcom. Felyne life simulator "MONHAN-NIKKI POKAPOKA AIROU-MURA (PSP)" is released by Capcom. September: Announces a joint business venture with Namco Bandai Games.
  • 2011 - August: FromSoftware works with Capcom. Felyne life simulator "MONHAN-NIKKI POKAPOKA AIROU-MURA G (PSP)" is released by Capcom. September: Develops the action RPG "DARK SOULS (PS3)" jointly with Namco Bandai Games. It sells 1.5 million units worldwide as of November 2011.
  • 2012 - January: Develops a game jointly with Namco Bandai Games and releases the mech action game "ARMORED CORE Ⅴ (PS3/Xbox 360)." March: FromSoftware works with Namco Bandai Games. A mech action game "KIDO-SENSHI GUNDAM UC (PS3)" is released by Namco Bandai Games. June: FromSoftware works with Capcom. Dramatic war simulator (vehicle simulator) "JUTEKKI (Xbox 360 Kinect)" is released by Capcom. October: DARK SOULS DLC and the DLC-bundled package "DARK SOULS with ARTORIAS OF THE ABYSS EDITION (PS3)" are released.
  • 2013 - September: Develops a game jointly with Namco Bandai Games and releases the mech action game "ARMORED CORE VERDICT DAY (PS3/Xbox 360)."
  • 2014 - March: Develops a game jointly with Namco Bandai Games and releases the action RPG "DARK SOULS Ⅱ (PS3/Xbox 360/Windows)" which ships 2.4 million units worldwide as of September 2014. April: Announces that KADOKAWA CORPORATION acquires an 80 per cent of From Software's stock. May: Becomes a wholly owned subsidiary of the Kadokawa group.
  • 2015 - February: Releases "DARK SOULS Ⅱ: SCHOLAR OF THE FIRST SIN (PS3/Xbox 360/Windows)" which bundles the base game and all three DLCs. Enters the SONY PlayStation 4 software market. March: The action RPG "Bloodborne (PS4)" which is developed by FromSoftware is released by Sony Interactive Entertainment. Enters the Microsoft Xbox One software market. April: Releases the remastered and updated version of "DARK SOULS Ⅱ: SCHOLAR OF THE FIRST SIN (PS4/Xbox One/Windows)." Enters the Nintendo 3DS software market. September: The Felyne life simulator "MONHAN-NIKKI POKAPOKA AIROU-MURA DX (3DS)" which is developed by FromSoftware is released by Capcom. October: Opens a new studio in Fukuoka to enhance development capacity. December: The action RPG "Bloodborne: The Old Hunters Edition (PS4)" which bundles the base game and the DLC developed by FromSoftware is released by Sony Interactive Entertainment.
  • 2016 - March: Releases "DARK SOULS Ⅲ (PS4/Xbox One/Windows)" as a joint development with Bandai Namco Entertainment. (April for the Windows version) It sells 3 million units worldwide as of April 2016.
  • 2017 - April: Releases "DARK SOULS Ⅲ THE FIRE FADES EDITION" which bundles the base game and all two DLCs.
  • 2018 - May: Releases "DARK SOULS REMASTERED" version of the original "DARK SOULS" (PS4/Xbox One/Windows). October: Releases Nintendo Switch version of "DARK SOULS REMASTERED". November: The VR Adventure "Déraciné (PS4/PSVR)" which is developed by FromSoftware is released by Sony Interactive Entertainment.
  • 2019 - March: Releases "SEKIRO: SHADOWS DIE TWICE (PS4/Xbox One/Windows)" as a joint development with Activision. It sells 2 million units worldwide as 10 days from release. August: Releases "METAL WOLF CHAOS" upgraded port of the original "METAL WOLF CHAOS XD" (PS4/Xbox One/Windows).
  • 2020 - May: Action RPG "DARK SOULS Ⅲ", a joint development with Bandai Namco Entertainment, ships a total of over 10 million units worldwide. The cumulative total units shipped for the "DARK SOULS" series also exceeds 27 million units.
  • 2022 - February: Releases "ELDEN RING (PS4/PS5/Xbox One/Xbox Series X|S/Windows)" as a joint development with Bandai Namco Entertainment. March: "ELDEN RING (PS4/PS5/Xbox One/Xbox Series X|S/Windows)" sells 1 million units in Japan, 12 million units worldwide. September: Third-party allotment to Sixjoy Hong Kong Limited and Sony Interactive Entertainment Inc.
  • 2023 - February: "ELDEN RING (PS4/PS5/Xbox One/Xbox Series X|S/Windows)" sells 20 million units worldwide. Press Release: BANDAI NAMCO Entertainment Inc. August: Releases "ARMORED CORE VI FIRES OF RUBICON (PS4/PS5/Xbox One/Xbox Series X|S/Windows)" as a joint development with Bandai Namco Entertainment. September: "SEKIRO: SHADOWS DIE TWICE (PS4/Xbox One/Windows)" sells 10 million units worldwide.
  • 2024 - June: ELDEN RING DLC and the DLC-bundled package "ELDEN RING SHADOW OF THE ERDTREE EDITION (PS4/PS5)" released. Over 5 million units sold worldwide 3 days after release.
